Why TummyTime?

Because it's not fair that Teddy gets to have all the fun, make some mummy time for tummy time!

Your Body Deserves Better

Becoming a mother changes everything - including your relationship with your body. TummyTime isn't just another fitness app; it's a companion created by Uncle Renches who understand that recovery isn't about "bouncing back" - it's about moving forward with confidence and strength.

  • Your body did something amazing
    Our approach honors the journey your body has been through, focusing on healing before performance. We focus on fitness for moms who are too tired to care but still kinda care, because emotional baggage shouldn't feel so heavy.
  • Workouts shorter than your baby’s nap
    Real-world workouts designed for interrupted sleep, baby naps, and the chaos of new motherhood, because your core shouldn’t be as broken as your sleep schedule.
  • Because mental wellness matters too
    Carrying a baby shouldn’t be your only workout, movement that nurtures both your body and mind during this transformative time. We want to help mums find their abs (and sanity) one workout at a time.
  • Absurdly tired? Absurdly strong
    Join thousands of mothers supporting each other as we rediscover our strength on our terms, at our own pace, with exercises that evolve as we do. Besides, stronger core means fewer emotional breakdowns* (hopefully).
  • Rebuilding yourself from the inside out
    We focus on strength where it matters—core, pelvic floor, and everything in between—so your body works for you, not against you, because sitting down shouldn't be a workout and sneezing SHOULD NOT be an extreme sport.

*Terms & Conditions Apply. Always consult with your healthcare provider parents before beginning any exercise program.
